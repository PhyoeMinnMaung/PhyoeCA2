Ireland Student Counselling Service

Language Programs

If you are not interested in attending a degree course but want to experience student life in Ireland or improve your English skills through both classroom instruction and daily life communication, language programs in Ireland are the best choice for you.

Benefits
  • Obtain a language school student visa for 8 months (6 months of study and 2 months of holidays).
  • Work 20 hours per week during study periods and 40 hours per week during holidays.
  • Enjoy student benefits, such as transportation discounts with a Student Leap Card.
  • Extend the course up to two more times after completing the initial 8-month program.
  • Affordable school fees.
